## Description (4,000 chars max)

Aski IQ is the complete field operations platform for industrial contractors, construction firms, and site services companies. Built for how crews actually work — offline-first, role-aware, and connected end-to-end from the first lead to the final invoice.

**FIELD OPERATIONS**
Clock in and out from your phone. Daily job reports auto-fill with site weather. Pre-work safety checklists, incident reports, and hazard logs are signed and audit-hashed on the spot. Crew timesheets flow straight to the approval queue — no paper, no re-entry.

**COMMERCIAL PIPELINE**
Estimating, quoting, invoicing, and change orders all live in one place. Build a detailed estimate from your product and service library, convert it to a polished PDF quote in one tap, and track it from draft to accepted. Manage RFIs, project budgets, and subcontract compliance — all linked to the same job.

**CRM & BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT**
Follow every lead from first contact to won project. Log calls, meetings, and emails against the opportunity. Create a quote directly from a won deal. The activity timeline shows your full relationship history with every client.

**SAFETY & COMPLIANCE**
Worker certification tracking flags expired and expiring tickets before they become a site entry problem. Incident reports capture type, severity, witnesses, and corrective actions — with WCB/OHS reportable flags and a tamper-evident audit hash. An enterprise diagnostic suite runs 25 real-time compliance checks at any time.

**PROCUREMENT**
Material requests flow through an approval → PO → delivery workflow. Supplier master keeps your preferred vendors, pricing notes, and account numbers in one place. Purchase orders generate from approved requests with a single tap.

**OFFLINE-FIRST SYNC**
Every record writes locally first. Sync pushes to Supabase when you're back online. Your crew keeps working in the field whether they have LTE or not — their entries land in the office queue automatically.

**ROLE-BASED ACCESS**
Executive, Manager, Project Manager, Office Admin, Estimator, Foreman, and Field Worker roles each see and do exactly what their job requires. Nothing more, nothing less.

**WHAT'S INCLUDED**
• Project management with budget vs. actual tracking
• Crew scheduling with conflict detection
• Daily timesheets and payroll export (CSV)
• Form builder — custom safety and field forms
• Estimating with cost codes, overhead, and profit margins
• Quote PDFs with scope, exclusions, and line items
• Invoice aging and payment tracking
• Change orders and RFIs
• Procurement — material requests and purchase orders
• Supplier master
• Incident reporting with regulatory flags
• Worker certification compliance tracker
• Daily job reports with weather integration
• Equipment registry with service due dates
• CRM pipeline — leads, contacts, opportunities, tasks, activities
• AI-powered field document drafting
• Widget dashboard — modular, role-aware, customizable
